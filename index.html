FAQ

What services do you offer?

We provide product development, UI/UX design, AI integration, cybersecurity, ongoing support, and technology consulting for entrepreneurs and startups.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes. We frequently help founders validate ideas and ship MVPs quickly while laying a scalable foundation for growth.

How do you handle cybersecurity?

Security is built-in from day one: secure coding practices, encryption, dependency hygiene, automated checks, and periodic reviews.

How can I get started?

Share your goals via the contact form — we’ll reply with next steps, a rough approach, and timeline.

What is a typical timeline for an MVP?

Most MVPs are delivered within 4–8 weeks depending on scope, integrations, and compliance requirements.

What tech stack do you use?

We choose the stack to fit your goals; common choices include modern web frameworks, cloud-native infrastructure, and proven security tooling.

How do you price projects?

We offer both fixed-price for well-defined scopes and time-and-materials for iterative work; after a short discovery we provide a transparent estimate.

Who owns the IP and source code?

You retain 100% ownership of the IP and source code; we hand over repositories, documentation, and access at each milestone.

Do you sign an NDA?

Yes — we can sign a mutual NDA before discussing details.

Do you provide ongoing support and SLAs?

Yes — we offer maintenance and support plans with response-time SLAs aligned to your needs.
